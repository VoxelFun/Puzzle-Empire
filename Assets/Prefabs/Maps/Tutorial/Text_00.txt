Select unit to begin move.
If you are close enough, you can select enemy's unit. This will begin fight between yours and enemy's units.

There are seven diffrent types of gems on the board.
Red, Blue, Green, Yellow and Purple gems are used to cast spells. White gem gives one random gem type that is used by your unit.
Skull gems are use to deal damage.
To cast a spell, click on gems status(under your unit) and select spell(if you hold, you will see spell description). Remember that you have to have enough gems collected.
